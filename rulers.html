Example progressions

A- B-7b5 C D- E- F G A-

CM7 D-7 E-7 FM7 G7 A-7 B-7b5 CM7

Tips

Root notes by themselves are assumed major, ex: A

Use - or m for minor, ex: A- Em

Use M7 for major 7, ex: CM7

Use 7 for dominant 7, ex: G7
